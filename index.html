Thank you for visiting my research profile.

I am a psychologist-turned-computer scientist, currently pursuing a PhD at the IT University of Copenhagen and the Pioneer Centre for Artificial Intelligence.

My passion is in the intersection of data-driven learning and psychology, especially with regards to the mental health domain. My PhD is centered around multi-modal machine learning for diagnostics and treatment.

My present project concerns non-verbal synchrony analysis during psychotherapy, attempting to model this openTSLM.
